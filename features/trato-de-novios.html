Trato de Novios.

A man and a woman find each other and fall in love.

Now, what could go wrong with that?

Let me tell you.

Men only wanted one thing from her. That's been the case since she was sixteen. And they were only good for one thing in return.

He tried to show her he was different. He tried to be different. He tried to be a hero instead of a slob.

They turned heads in the supermarket. The department store. The gas station.

The weeds that grow along the curb seem so much taller after it rains. Sometimes, hope makes a comeback or at least a cameo.

There was only one problem.

This man and this woman—they met in a brothel. A massage parlour. Yeah, those things are legal in Mexico.

Is that a problem? Why? Why is it a problem?

And if it's not a problem—why not? How can it not be?
